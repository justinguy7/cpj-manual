# Tool Kit

The following is the recommended tool kit.

* Torx driver set: T9, T10
* Phillips #1 screwdriver 
* Flathead screwdriver
* Metric hex key set: 1.5, 2, 2.5, 3, 4 mm
* Metric hex key with ball-end: 3 mm
* SAE hex key: 3/32 inch
* Lens brush
* Socket wrench (1/4" drive)
* Socket set: 14 mm spark plug socket
* Crescent wrench
* Needle nose pliers
* Flush cutter
* Torque wrench: 0-15 in/lbs, 0-65 in/lbs, 10-150 in/lbs
* SMA torque wrench
* 8 mm wrench
* Threadlocker: Loctite 242, 272
* Adhesives: E6000, medium CA glue
* Zipties
* X-Acto knife
* Cables: USB-A to USB-C, USB-A to USB micro, FTDI to JST-GH, ethernet
* Flashlight
* Safety wire pliers
* Safety wire
* Torque stripe
